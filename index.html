About Me


Hi there, my name is Collin Alex Yarrington I'm a young passionate developer that enjoys creating user friendly and professional web applications. I have worked with many languages and I'm always eager to learn and apply my skills I have obtained over the past few years.


I was born and raised in South Africa Western Cape. I Developed an interest for all types of programming technologies from the age of twelve. My father introduced me to my first programming language which was JavaScript. It was at this point I had made a deal with my father that if I were to create anything using any programming language he would pay me 1000 rand... I didn't develop anything until I hit college (at this point up until now I had forgotten about the deal). Besides the deal it motivated me to explore my interest and help me figure out what I wanted to do for a living. My father is a software engineer and just watching the things he achieved by writing code, inspired me the most. When I hit college I was not really sure if this was the correct career choice for me, but I kept going and kept learning and it wasn't until the second year of college that made me 100% sure this is the right path for me in terms of career choice. I graduated with an "Adanced Certificate in Graphic and Web Development" at the 2019.


In my spare time I enjoy gaming but what I enjoy most about gaming is the many variations of personalities you come across that often lead to interesting conversations. When I find myself bored or not busy with anything I would set myself a programming challenge or I would request one from my friends or family to help me practice and test my knowlege. Those projects I post to my github repository. Check my stuff out ;)
